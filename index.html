Join Our Research Group!

I am seeking highly motivated and talented individuals to join us as Postdoctoral Fellows. We are passionate about pioneering next-generation materials and leveraging advanced computational tools to address global challenges in energy, environment, and beyond.

For Postdoctoral Fellows

  • A PhD degree in a relevant field.
  • Expertise in experimental materials synthesis/characterization OR computational materials science/AI/ML. Candidates with experience in both areas are highly encouraged to apply.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, and scientific writing skills.

What We Offer

  • A collaborative and well-equipped research environment.
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities and computational resources.
  • Opportunities for international collaborations and conference presentations.
  • A competitive salary and benefits package.

How to Apply

Interested candidates are encouraged to send the following documents to [yangjeongpark@kist.re.kr]:

  1. A Cover Letter detailing your research interests, relevant experience, and career goals. Please also mention how your interests align with our group's research areas.
  2. A detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV), including a list of publications (if applicable).
  3. Contact information for 2-3 References.

We encourage applications from all qualified individuals. Applications will be reviewed on an ongoing basis until the positions are filled.

We look forward to hearing from you and potentially welcoming you to our team!
